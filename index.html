The Band

Under the influence of prohibition-era speakeasies, this inspired 5 piece plays a delightful mix of 1920s and 30s hot jazz and early swing.

Imbued with a joyful exuberance, David McTea and the Swinging Magpies perform an intoxicating blend of period jazz standards and original material, lovingly crafted in the vintage style.

The band comprises:

  • David McTea: (vocals and baritone guitar)
  • Lucinda Dunne: (clarinet)
  • Vicky Cowles: (bassoon)
  • Gregory Madge: (guitar)
  • Caroline Scott: (percussion)

David is also an accomplished swing and jazz dancer, entertaining the masses and fully convinced that it don't mean a thing if it aint got that swing! Such gifts add an aura of excitement and showmanship to performances and David is always more than happy to provide a friendly and fun dance lesson to break the ice and get your night off to a swinging start.

Bringing an authentic jazz feel to any occasion, David McTea and the Swinging Magpies are available for public and private bookings, providing stylish live music and effortless sophistication to all manner of events, from weddings to corporate shindigs to private parties.
